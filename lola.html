Lola Julia


697 dead. One alive. Julia was the lone survivor in a brutal massacre that killed her family and ended her life as she knows it. Narrowly escaping death as a little girl, she swore to seek revenge on powerful political strongmen during a time when women are delegated to the sidelines. In a family epic lasting several generations, "Lola Julia" tells the story of how a woman rose to power through deceit, manipulation, and pure, unadulterated rage.
