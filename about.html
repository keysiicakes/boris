About Us

Hirayanthologies was founded by a group of college students to give first-time writers, young adults, and fellow students literary opportunities to share their passion through writing, and publishing their work through traditional print.


Still in its development phase, Hirayanthologies offers the world a fresh perspective on student visions.


Mission

The Filipino youth empowered to share their passion through writing and other artistic pursuits


Vision

Being the means of which knowledge, passion, and ideas are shared.
