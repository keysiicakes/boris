Petsa Diyes: Nang Mabura ang Maynila


Windows shattered. Then everything crumbled down. August 10. A series of coordinated terrorist bombings wiped out Manila from the map, with more than half of the city's population perishing on an incident later known as "Petsa Diyes." What was left of Barangay 105 had to pick up their lives from the ruins while staving off hunger, grief, corruption, police violence, and possibly an international cover-up.
